(1) üòÄ Design Physical Performance Assessment Tool

Master thesis: "Accuracy of a modified Timed Up and Go test in differentiating fallers and non-fallers among community dwelling elderly"

Hui Tian originately designs a physical performance test whereby clinician can quickly examine dynamic stability of older adults during walking, hence individuals with fall risk can be identified. Similar to previous study in this area, fall history is collected as outcome while perfromance of test is used as classification criterion. ROC (receiver operating characteristic curve) and AUC (area under curve) are conducted to examine validation of this new test.

Creatively, Hui Tian tries to develop a mathematical formula to describe subjects' performance. She havs tried to used Logistic regression method to fit data.
This study will be finished in February, 2021.

(2) üòÄUsing machine learning method and motion capture data to develop a prediction tool of fall

In this study, Hui Tian collects:
Kinematic data of walking ( step width, length, joint angle, and speed etc)
Medical history (pian, osteoarthritis etc)
Fall history (retrospective and prospective fall)

Once finishing data extraction, Machine learning algorithms will be performed to create fall prediction tool. This study will be finished December,2020.
